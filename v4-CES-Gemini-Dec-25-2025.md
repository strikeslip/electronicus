## 1. Buchla (Series 100/200/Music Easel)

* **History & Builder:** Created by **Don Buchla** in 1963 in San Francisco. It pioneered "West Coast Synthesis," emphasizing complex timbres over traditional melodic structures.
* **Prominent Artists & YouTube References:**
* **Suzanne Ciani:** "The Diva of the Diode" and master of the Buchla 200.
* Suzanne Ciani - Buchla 200: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6864S_SNoD0](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.youtube.com/watch%3Fv%3D6864S_SNoD0)


* **Caterina Barbieri:** Utilizes the Buchla 200e for intricate, polyphonic patterns.
* Caterina Barbieri Live at Barbican: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3S_yXp0_9_E](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.youtube.com/watch%3Fv%3D3S_yXp0_9_E)




* **Software Versions:** Arturia Buchla Easel V, Buchla 700 (id700 iOS).
* **Javascript & Code Examples:**
* **Buchla-style Low Pass Gate JS:** [https://github.com/monomono-vst/buchla-lpg-js](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://github.com/monomono-vst/buchla-lpg-js)


* **Supercollider & Github:**
* **SynthDefPool (Buchla modules):** [https://github.com/supercollider-quarks/SynthDefPool](https://github.com/supercollider-quarks/SynthDefPool)

## 2. Korg M1

* **History & Builder:** Released by **Korg** in 1988. It is the world’s best-selling digital synthesizer, establishing the "workstation" category.

## 3. Yamaha DX7

## 4. Moog Minimoog

* **History & Builder:** Designed by **Bill Hemsath** and **Robert Moog** in 1970. It was the first synthesizer designed to be portable and sold in retail stores.
* **Prominent Artists & YouTube References:**
* **Kraftwerk:** Created the defining basslines for the track "Autobahn."
* Kraftwerk - Autobahn: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x-G28iyPtz0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x-G28iyPtz0)


* **Wendy Carlos:** Transitioned from the Moog Modular to the Minimoog for live versatility.
* Wendy Carlos Interview: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=au_yidmC8oY](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.youtube.com/watch%3Fv%3Dau_yidmC8oY)




* **Software Versions:** Moog Model D App, Arturia Mini V, Softube Model 72.

## 5. Sequential Prophet-5

## 7. EMS VCS3

* The Knife - Full of Fire: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W10F0ezctIQ](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.youtube.com/watch%3Fv%3DW10F0ezctIQ)
